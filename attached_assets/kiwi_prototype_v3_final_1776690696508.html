Chicken & Black Bean Pasta
25 min · Easy · serves 4
480
Calories
38g
Protein
44g
Carbs
14g
Fat
Ingredients
Chicken breasts
1 lb
Pasta (penne or rigatoni)
12 oz
Black beans, drained
1 can
Canned diced tomatoes
1 can
Yellow onion, diced
1 medium
Olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper staples
—
Steps
1
Boil salted water and cook pasta to package directions. Reserve ¼ cup pasta water.
2
Season chicken with salt and pepper. Cook in 1 Tbsp olive oil over medium-high, 6 min per side. Slice.
3
Sauté onion in same pan 3 min. Add garlic 1 min. Add tomatoes and black beans, simmer 5 min.
4
Toss pasta into sauce with splash of pasta water. Top with sliced chicken. Serve.
